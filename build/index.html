Hand Cricket.io

Hand cricket.io is a free realtime multiplayer hand cricket online website game which makes hand cricket game more interesting

We all have played and enjoyed hand cricket atleast once with our friends in our childhood days. In this digitalized world, we are trying to give you the same level of experience through Hand cricket.io. We are improving the gaming experience through audio, animations,trophies, hand cricket cup tournamets with orange and purple cap and putting efforts to make the game more likeable

How to Play Hand cricket .io?

  • First select mode you want to play (play with a random person or a friend) in Hand cricket.io .
  • Once you connected with your friend or a random person, you may toss the coin while your opponent calls for the toss(heads or tails) or vice versa.
  • Once the toss gets completed,you may get a chance to choose to bat or bowl depending on the toss result.
  • After choosing, if you are
    1. Batting - Select a number which you think opponent won't select,so that you can score runs
    2. Bowling - Select a number which you think opponent will select,so that you can take the opponent's wicket
  • If you are playing a random game, at the end of the match, you can contribute points to your favourite team depending upon the match results. This event doesn't apply to the "Play with the Friend" mode
